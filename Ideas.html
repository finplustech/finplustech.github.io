INFINITY-CHAIN

The Infinity-Chain is a technology that creates a new blockchain every time the current chain has met its limit of 50GB's of storage. This way all electronic devices has the chance to download the current chain directly since it always is less than 50GB's. The system will allow for High capacity blocks and fast blocks, which will greatly improve scalability. Every time the current chain reaches 50GB's, the system creates a new chain by taking a snapshot of the current one and a new blockchain is now in use. The old one stops completely and is stored in chain storage localy and within nodes. They are only used as receipts so that there still is proof of old transactions. 

Fast & Scalable

High capacity blocks and fast confirmation times allows for a highly scalable blockchain that can be used for normal tasks daily.

Largely compatible & Safe

Since the blockchain never is larger than 50GB's, the chain can be downloaded by most devices and is therefore safe to use as no thirdparty is needed.

Chain storage for Receipts

Chain storage in nodes and localy, for proof of old transactions.
